Need help?

  1. Go to your Zoom homepage(
    <your-domain(optional)>.zoom.us
    )
  2. Look at the sidebar. Find and click on "Reports"
  3. Look for the text that says "Usage". Click on it.
  4. Filter the meetings by the date filter to find your desired meetings.
  5. When you find a meeting you would like to use, look at the number that is the second to last text on the row containing your meeting. It should be blue, and underlined when you hover over it. Click on it.
  6. Check the checkbox next to "Show unique users", and make sure not to check the one next to "Export with meeting data"
  7. Click the "Export" button in the top right corner of the pop-up.
  8. Go to the file upload(click this text to jump to the file upload).
  9. Click on the button that says "Choose Files".
  10. Select the files you would like to upload. To select multiple files, hold down the CTRL(Windows) or CMD(iOS/Mac) key while you click on your desired files.
  11. Click the "Select"/"Choose"/"Open" button next to the "Cancel" button.
  12. Go to the search box(click this text to jump to the search box), and type in a name.
  13. However many instances of the desired name show up is how many meetings that person has attended(under that name) out of all the meetings you selected.
